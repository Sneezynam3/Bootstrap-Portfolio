About Me

I graduated Colorado State University with a bachelors in Communication Studies. I have worked a plethora of jobs since. In the supply chain department I ran a liquor store, worked in a warehouse, a greenhouse, a trucking yard, and made malt for Coors Brewery. In the customer service sector I caddied, worked fast food, and held the service specialist title in an office setting. My transition to tech has led me to study html and css through Skillcrush, then a JavaScript prep course at Galvanize before ending up in the University of Denvers bootcamp.
